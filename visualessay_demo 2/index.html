About the project

Over the past five years I have worked in the healthcare, pharmaceutical industry. I have always tried to understand healthcare costs and their implications. I am always interested in finding out where healthcare costs come from. The viewpoint I took with this project was trying to find the connection or trends between various different attributes. Does your gender, family status, weight, habits, etc. effect your costs? I decided to create a visual essay for this project because data analytics is all about bringing users along the data journey with you. Visual essays resonate really well with me and I think they are an effective way to demonstrate different aspects of a set of data and help the user to understand one step at a time. Since the data was simulated it was hard to correlate specific findings with realistic thoughts, but using the data given I found that region does matter. There is a distinct difference between those who do smoke and those who do not smoke as far as BMI is concerned and overall costs.. Gender also plays a big part in costs and if you have 2-3 children your costs are more likely to be higher.
